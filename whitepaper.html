BHARAT USD (BUSD)

Official Whitepaper v1.0

"Stability. Utility. Scalability."

1. Abstract

Bharat USD (BUSD) is a decentralized digital asset built on the Arbitrum L2 Network. It aims to bridge the gap between traditional Indian finance and the blockchain ecosystem. By focusing on low transaction costs and high-speed settlement, BUSD provides a stable alternative for digital payments within the Indian subcontinent.

2. The Vision: Road to ₹80.00+

While BUSD launches at an entry-level rate of ₹0.90, our core strategy is focused on value appreciation through scarcity and real-world adoption.

Value Drivers:
  • Supply Scarcity: 2% Burn mechanism on every transaction.
  • Institutional Demand: Integration with Indian FinTech apps.
  • Limited Circulation: Only 10 Million total supply.

4. Real-World Utility

Unlike speculative tokens, BUSD is being integrated into the following services:

  • Mobile Recharges: Instant top-ups for all major Indian carriers.
  • Bill Payments: Electricity, Water, and Broadband payments using BUSD.
  • P2P Marketplace: A secure platform for direct user-to-user exchange with Admin Escrow.

6. Security

BUSD leverages the security of the Ethereum Mainnet through the Arbitrum Rollup technology. Our P2P Market uses a strict Escrow Mechanism where tokens are held by the BUSD Official Team until payment is verified, ensuring 100% safety for buyers and sellers.
